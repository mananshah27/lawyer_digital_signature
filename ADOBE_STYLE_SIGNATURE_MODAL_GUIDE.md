# Adobe-Style Signature Modal Guide

## Overview

The Adobe-Style Signature Modal is a new component that provides a comprehensive signature creation experience with a signature pad on the left side and contact details form on the right side. This creates professional signatures that include all your contact information, making them perfect for business documents.

## Features

### Left Panel - Signature Creation
- **Signature Method Toggle**: Choose between "Draw Signature" and "Type Signature"
- **Signature Pad**: Draw your signature using mouse or touch
- **Signature Preview**: See how your final signature will look
- **Signature Name**: Give your signature a descriptive name

### Right Panel - Contact Details
- **Personal Information**: Full name, email, phone
- **Professional Information**: Job title, department, company, location
- **Password Protection**: Secure your signature with a password

## How It Works

### For Drawn Signatures
1. Select "Draw Signature" mode
2. Use your mouse or touch to draw your signature
3. Fill in your contact details on the right
4. The system will combine your drawn signature with all the contact information
5. Result: A complete signature block with your handwritten signature and professional details

### For Typed Signatures
1. Select "Type Signature" mode
2. Enter your full name
3. Fill in your contact details on the right
4. The system will generate a professional text-based signature
5. Result: A complete signature block with your name and all professional details

## What Gets Applied to PDF

When you apply this signature to a PDF, you'll see:
- Your name (drawn or typed)
- Company name
- Job title and department
- Location
- Email and phone
- Timestamp

## Usage Instructions

1. **Open the Modal**: Click the "Adobe-Style Signature Modal" button in the sidebar
2. **Choose Method**: Select either "Draw Signature" or "Type Signature"
3. **Create Signature**: 
   - Draw your signature OR type your name
   - Fill in all contact details
   - Set a password for security
4. **Save**: Click "Save Signature" to create your signature
5. **Apply**: Use the signature in the signature panel to apply it to PDFs

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Why don't I see my contact details on the PDF?**
A: Make sure you've filled in the contact details form on the right side before saving. The signature combines both the signature and contact information.

**Q: Can I use my drawn signature with contact details?**
A: Yes! When you choose "Draw Signature" mode, your drawn signature will be combined with all the contact details you enter.

**Q: How do I change the signature style?**
A: The current version uses a clean, professional style. For more customization options, use the original "Adobe-Style Signature" component.

## Integration

This modal is fully integrated with the existing signature system:

- Works with the existing signature positioning system
